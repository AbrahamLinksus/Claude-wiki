## Registration Requirements

- **No Copilot subscription required** to sit the exam — the official page lists no subscription as a prerequisite.
- You need a **personal Microsoft (MSA) account** to register. Avoid using a work/school AAD account — exam records are lost if you leave that organization.
- Pay the exam fee (price varies by country, billed through Pearson Vue).
- For hands-on practice while studying, GitHub offers a **free Copilot tier** with limited monthly completions — sufficient to get practical experience without a paid plan.

## Exam Tips

- The exam was significantly rewritten in January 2026 — new topics include Agent Mode, MCP, Sub-Agents, Spaces, and Spark
- Most questions cover GA features; some Preview features appear if widely used
- Score 700+ to pass; retake allowed after 24 hours on first failure
